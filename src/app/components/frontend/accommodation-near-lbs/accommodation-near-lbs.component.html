Primrose Hill

This beautiful area takes its name after the “Primrose Hill”, adjacents to London Zoo, just North of Regent’s Park, between St John’s Wood, Swiss Cottage and Hampstead. It has a quaint, picturesque character with its rows of pastel-coloured Victorian houses and terraces, which make it popular amongst families, young professionals and within the artistic community.
