Rental search experience is broken…

Looking for a property to rent in London or any other big city? Be prepared to spend a lot of time on multiple websites, online ads, talking to estate agents, visiting often nonsensical properties, etc.

Do you know for a large number of tenants it can take between 50 and 250 hours to find a property?

…Requiring a new approach for property search…

In the modern world where technology enables numerous on-demand services, we are introducing our Artificial Intelligence-powered app that enables you to ‘find & finalise’ a property efficiently!

Need more help? Hire a Sherpa who will do all the heavy lifting for you.

…A product that works for tenants!

Discover neighbourhoods and properties that match your lifestyle, discuss and decide with your friends and family, set viewings directly from the app! And lots more new features coming soon to make rental search even easier.

We put in the hard work so you don’t waste your time anymore!
